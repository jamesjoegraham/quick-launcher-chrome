Quick Site Launcher Setup

Enter the full URL for the websites you want to open with a keyboard shortcut. **Remember to set the actual shortcut keys on the `chrome://extensions/shortcuts` page after installation!**

How to Use:

  1. Enter the full URLs for the 4 slots above and click "Save".
  2. Go to chrome://extensions/shortcuts.
  3. Find "Quick Site Launcher" and assign your desired key combinations (e.g., Ctrl+Shift+1) to the four "Opens Custom Site X" commands.
  4. Use the keyboard shortcut from any Chrome tab to open the corresponding website!
